Welcome to The Anam

Mui Ne

Nestled within lush foliage, The Anam gently cascades towards the azure waters of the East Sea along Mui Ne’s famed honey-hued coastline. Discover the latest luxury resort addition to The Anam collection and the only Small Luxury Hotels of the World™ affiliated member in Vietnam that promises all the splendour, romance and nostalgia of old-world Indochine charm infused with the graciousness inherent in Vietnamese hospitality.

Indochine Finesse
Our 127 stylish rooms and suites recall the warmth and luxury of a bygone era. The design ethos pays homage to the craftsmanship of old, fusing Vietnamese and Indochine artisanal elements with indigenous materials, customised antique-style tiling and natural colour palettes. Passion for the arts that depict scenes of Vietnamese life is evident with paintings by local artists adorning the walls.

Finding Happiness

The Anam Mui Ne sits at the heart of a vibrant natural landscape that appeals to multi-generations with diverse beauty and experiences. Whether seeking an adrenalin rush through exhilarating water sports pursuits, immersive mind-body offerings through wellness programmes and gastronomy, or discovering attractions during excursions, we cater to every guest with little ones happily entertained at our kids’ club.
Feast
Whether your tastes are for gourmet delicacies, age-old home-style cooking, or down-to-earth pleasures of street food, dining experiences with us will tempt and sometimes surprise you.
Mui Ne Dining
Wellness
The Anam Spa is a tranquil sanctuary where ancient Asian healing traditions interweave into holistic wellness journeys designed to recalibrate and rejuvenate body, mind and spirit.
The Anam Spa
Kids’ Club
Our dedicated kids’ club offers a range of supervised activities for children aged between four to 12 years. Let your little ones make new friends during creative fun, gaming stations, and indoor or outdoor scheduled activities – all under the watchful eye of our caring team. An adult must accompany any children below the age of four years.
Excursions
Step beyond our resort and discover Mui Ne as a bona fide adventurer’s haven with historical vestiges of the past Champa Kingdom that once ruled south-central Vietnam.
Excursions
Yoga
Reconnect with your natural flow through the power of yoga. Join our dedicated yogi for complimentary group classes in the mornings at our yoga room or enquire about private sessions, subject to availability.
Swimming Pools
Alternate between our freshwater and saltwater swimming pools for a splashing fun time. Swim energising laps or relax on sun loungers to work on your tan as you stay hydrated with refreshments.
Fitness Centre
Maintain your fitness regime with our air-conditioned facility overlooking the tropical landscape. Our fitness centre comes fully equipped with state-of-the-art Precor technology, including cardiovascular training machines, treadmills, bench presses and free weights.
The Anam Boutique
Discover a collection of classic and contemporary pearl and gemstone designs handcrafted by master jewellers at Vietnam’s leading pearl jewellery brand, featuring Tahitian, South Sea, Akoya and Mabe pearls. Find your perfect souvenir, or pick out essential items as you browse.
